This section contains interviews with elderly Deaf signers of Catalan Sign Language (LSC), German Sign Language (DGS), Italian Sign Language (LIS), Sign Language of the Netherlands (NGT), Spanish Sign Language (LSE), and Turkish Sign Language (TİD). It also contains interviews and historical materials from French Sign Language (LSF) and Israeli Sign Language (ISL).
These materials reflect the life experiences of Deaf individuals, ranging from personal memories to historical events from a Deaf perspective.
